Product Types

Four Roofing Families, One Complete System

Corrugated sheets to stone-coated steel — every roofing type.

ECONOMICAL

Corrugated Metal Sheets

Galvanized and Alu-Zinc coated steel sheets in standard corrugated profiles. Lightweight, fast to install, and extremely cost-effective. The workhorse of industrial and agricultural roofing worldwide.

ARCHITECTURAL

Standing Seam Panels

Concealed fastener system with clean, continuous lines. PVDF color coating in 30+ standard colors. Ideal for architect-designed projects requiring a modern, sleek roofline with exceptional weather performance.

PREMIUM

Stone-Coated Steel Tiles

Steel core with natural stone chip coating — the look of traditional clay or slate tiles with the strength and light weight of steel. Class A fire rating, Class 4 impact resistance, 50-year warranty available.

RESIDENTIAL

Asphalt Shingles

Fiberglass-reinforced asphalt shingles with ceramic-coated mineral granules. Available in 3-tab, architectural, and designer styles. The most popular residential roofing material in North America and growing globally.

WATERPROOF

Waterproofing Membranes

APP/SBS modified bitumen and PVC/TPO single-ply membranes for flat roofs, podiums, and below-grade waterproofing. Torch-applied, self-adhesive, and mechanically fastened options available.
