Product Types

Four Product Lines, One Quality Standard

Each product line engineered for specific outdoor applications. Uniform factory-direct pricing across all types.

BESTSELLER

WPC Solid Decking

Full-profile solid WPC board with realistic wood grain texture. 140×25mm standard, 2.2m / 2.9m / 5.8m lengths. UV-stabilized, slip-resistant surface. Ideal for high-end residential and hospitality projects.

LIGHTWEIGHT

WPC Hollow Decking

Chambered hollow profile reduces weight by 35% while maintaining structural integrity. 140×23mm, grooved surface for enhanced grip. Cost-effective solution for large-scale commercial projects.

PREMIUM

Co-extrusion Decking

Advanced 360° polymer shield technology — the outer layer provides superior scratch, stain, and UV resistance. 25-year warranty on structural integrity. The ultimate choice for demanding outdoor environments.

VERSATILE

Outdoor Cladding & Fencing

Complete outdoor wall and fencing system — WPC cladding panels, composite fence boards, and matching accessories. Weather-proof, termite-proof, zero painting required.
