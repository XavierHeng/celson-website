Product Types

Calcium Silicate Board Variants

Three grades: fire-rated, moisture-proof, exterior cladding.

Standard

Standard Calcium Silicate

General-purpose fire-rated board for interior partition walls and suspended ceilings. A1 fire rating, 6–12mm thickness.

High-Density

High-Density Calcium Silicate

Extra-dense formulation for exterior cladding and load-bearing applications. Superior impact resistance, 8–12mm.

Fiber-Reinforced

Fiber-Reinforced Calcium Silicate

Cellulose/glass fiber reinforced for maximum flexural strength. Ideal for curved walls and wet-area applications, 6–10mm.
