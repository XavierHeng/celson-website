Product Types

Four Essential Categories, One Supplier

Stock everything your installers need. Consistent quality, bulk pricing, consolidated shipping.

ESSENTIAL

Joint Compounds

Ready-mix and powder joint compounds for seamless drywall finishing. Smooth application, minimal shrinkage, excellent adhesion. Available in 20kg and 25kg packaging.

HIGH-VOLUME

Screws & Fasteners

Drywall screws (black phosphate), self-drilling screws (zinc plated), and specialty fasteners for metal framing. Box-packed for easy jobsite distribution.

PRECISION

Tapes & Corner Beads

Paper joint tapes, fiberglass mesh tapes (self-adhesive), galvanized steel corner beads, and PVC corner beads with mesh wings. Perfect corners every time.

STRUCTURAL

Metal Profiles & Channels

Galvanized steel studs, tracks, furring channels, and ceiling T-bars. Full range of gauges and lengths for any framing system.
